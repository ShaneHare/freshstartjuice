Batch 11 Ginger Boost — R&D Formulation
🟡 R&D Formulation
Focus
  • Developed in response to customer feedback requesting a stronger ginger-forward flavor profile
  • Target output of approximately 120 oz (3.5 L) while maintaining balance and drinkability
  • Added cucumber to increase yield, provide hydration, and soften the overall profile
  • Retained lemons to deliver brightness and a clean finish
  • Evaluating vanilla and star anise as signature finishing notes
Outcome
  • Exceeded target output — produced 135.3 fl oz (4.0 L)
  • Achieved 77.3% yield, strong process efficiency for an R&D run
  • Bold ginger-forward profile balanced by pineapple sweetness and cucumber hydration
  • Star anise and vanilla added post-juice to introduce a subtle warming finish
  • Future batches will evaluate replacing lemon with orange to refine acidity balance
