Production log

Batch notes

Every batch is documented — equipment, process, yield, what we learned. We track everything so every run is better than the last.

Batch 05 System Build
Nama J2 Cold Press
Focus
  • Produced Roots+ using same recipe and process — baseline consistency
  • Introduced new flavor: Roots Refresh (beet, watermelon, mint, lemon)
  • Completed first full bottling run (10 bottles)
  • Developed cleaning, bottling, and storage protocols
  • Established labeling system (2x4 format with QR feedback loop)
  • Integrated batch tracking and customer feedback workflow
Outcome
  • Maintained Roots+ consistency across multiple batches
  • Successfully introduced and produced Roots Refresh
  • Bottled first full run (10 units)
  • Cold chain held from prep through bottling and storage
  • Repeatable sanitation and workflow controls in place
  • Label includes product identity, ingredients, QR feedback, batch/date tracking
  • Identified improvement: add fluid oz (net volume) to label
  • Direct customer feedback loop enabled via QR + survey
  • Ready for initial product testing through controlled drop-offs
Takeaway

Established a consistent baseline with Roots+ while expanding the product line with Roots Refresh. This batch connects product, process, and feedback into a working system — moving from internal refinement to real-world validation.

Batch 04b Sanitation Protocol
Nama J2 Cold Press
Focus
  • Developed and tested cleaning + sanitation protocol
  • Established repeatable prep station standards
  • Validated clean workflow across multi-day production
  • Maintained product safety and quality control
Outcome
  • Standardized bleach + water sanitation process
  • Surfaces, sink, equipment cleaned before and after each stage
  • Clear separation between prep and bottling environments
  • Cold chain maintained from produce → juicing → storage
  • Improved confidence in food safety and consistency
  • No impact to workflow timing or batch efficiency (~50–60 min)
Protocol Snapshot
  • Day 1 Clean → wash produce → sanitize → refrigerate
  • Day 2 Pre-clean → prep → re-clean → juice → bottle → refrigerate
Takeaway

Established a repeatable sanitation protocol. This batch strengthens food safety, reduces risk, and supports scaling into a compliant operation.

Batch 04 Refinement
Nama J2 Cold Press
Focus
  • Recipe refinement — more lime, less ginger
  • Consistent ingredient weights across batches
  • Implemented cleaning and sanitation protocol
  • Improved workflow control (prep → clean → juice → bottle)
Outcome
  • ~58–60% yield (stable, slight variance from recipe changes)
  • ~50–60 min batch time (incl. cleaning + prep)
  • Slight output variance (~0.01L) with minimal transfer loss
  • Brighter, more balanced flavor profile
  • Smoother finish — reduced ginger spice
  • Maintained cold chain from prep through bottling
  • Improved process control and repeatability
Takeaway

Refined both product and process. This batch strengthens operational discipline and moves toward a consistent, production-ready workflow.

Batch 03 Benchmark
Nama J2 Cold Press
Focus
  • Consistent prep, flow and cleanup
  • Balanced ingredient combinations
  • Continued data tracking across every run
Outcome
  • ~34% yield — best to date
  • Consistent output (~2+ quarts per run)
  • Controlled pulp and minimal waste
Takeaway

Established a repeatable, data-backed process. This batch sets the current performance benchmark.

Batch 02 Transition
Nama J2 Cold Press — upgrade
Focus
  • Applied process improvements from Batch 01
  • Introduced true cold-press extraction
Outcome
  • Immediate improvement in yield
  • Cleaner juice and better separation
  • Reduced waste and rework
Takeaway

Key inflection point. Combining process discipline with the right equipment significantly improved performance.
